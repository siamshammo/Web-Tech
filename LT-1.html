The journey of a web developer begins with understanding the skeleton of the web: HTML5. It is essential to master the structure before moving on to styles or interactivity. Without a solid foundation, even the best CSS won't save a poorly structured document.

In this lab, I am practicing how to highlight important information using the mark tag. I am also including copyright information or fine print at the bottom of the page. HTML5 makes it easy to organize content logically for both users and search engines.
